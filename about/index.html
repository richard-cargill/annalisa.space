Key points of my design experience:

  • Design thinking approach, always user first
  • Web and Mobile experience
  • Shipping large scale projects
  • Working closely with stakeholders, eng, PO, marketers, helping them to ship products and to prioritise features
  • Fast mocking, prototyping and testing approach in order to collect feedback at the early stage of the process and iterate
  • Believing that communication and decision making are the key to make great team and help team member to build great products

My UI and UX background allows me to work on the most complex design challenges helping companies to launch products delivering the best in class user experience. Working closely with stakeholders and developers I help the teams to identify constrains at the very beginning. I'm able to organise design sprints and workshops helping stakeholders to identify design opportunities.

I share the vision of good design with Dieter Rams:

  1. Good design is innovative
  2. Good design makes a product useful
  3. Good design is aesthetic
  4. Good design makes a product understandable
  5. Good design is unobtrusive
  6. Good design is honest
  7. Good design is long-lasting
  8. Good design is thorough down to the last detail
  9. Good design is environmentally friendly
  10. Good design involves as little design as possible

Adding that:

  1. Good design is accessible.

My interests:

Outside the office I am busy reading, studying and working on projects regarding design and development. I am very interested in ethics of information and design, accessibility, inclusive design.

I am a member of the Jury at www.cssdesignawards.com and sometimes I like to write articles and to partecipate at hackathons. I'm also helping at the UXPA UK.

In my free time I like playing D&D, board games and learning drums.
